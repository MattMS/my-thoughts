Religion

I characterise myself as open agnostic. The "open" qualifier fits with my interest in learning more about all religions.

Films

These are a few films that shaped my views on religion.

Life of Pi

Life of Pi on Wikipedia

Pi's acceptance of different religions was inspirational and fits with my own curiousity.

Oh My God

Oh My God on Wikipedia

Interesting to see people concisely describe different beliefs.

It was also nice that the end message was mostly "God is love".

The Thirteenth Floor

The Thirteenth Floor on IMDB

If life can be a simulation, then there could be an intelligent creator.

Watching The Matrix before this may have also helped, but I did not realise this at the time.
